New era of Koxy AI

September 11, 2023 · 2 min read
Koxy Team
Koxy AI blogging team

We released a demo version of Koxy AI about a month ago and We got big support from the community, so We are taking the project a new level with this update to introduce the product We always wanted to introduce.

Infrastructure​

We upgraded the infrastructure of the APIs, databases, and connections for more secure and faster experience.

A new era needs a new look.​

New UX​

We have upgraded the overall user experience in Koxy AI to meet the needs of developers using the platform to build their projects. this comes with better alerts, flexibility, ease of use, and more...

New UI​

Koxy Database​

We are bringing big updates to Koxy database and now you have access to a production-ready No-SQL database in your Cloudspace with stable nodes to access the data from your API flows.

Real-time​

We added decentralized real-time features with no limits and zero config. Now you can build real-time applications using Koxy AI and Koxy JS library easily.

Deployments​

Now you can manage your Cloudspace's deployment, deployed variables, token, pause and resume your deployment and more...

Cloud functions​

Functions are serverless Javascript functions, distributed globally at the edge—close to your users. You can integrate functions into your flows or envoke them from your front-end

API runner​

We added an API flow runner so you can run your API flows in the cloud easily

New pricing plans​

We updated our pricing plans. learn more

Teams​

Team collaboration is now a thing in Koxy AI so you can bring your team with you.

Koxy JS​

Koxy JS got a major update with real-time features, and more options to rou your API flows and cloud functions

Much more​

We updated almost everything in Koxy AI to meet developer's needs to build a complete serverless back-end, and more is coming in the future.
